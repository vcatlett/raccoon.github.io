01

Drawbacks of Uber

Why services like Uber don't put the user first


Rideshare services like Uber have become more and more popular over the years. Despite this, only about 22 states have implemented pro-ridesharing guidelines to ensure safe operations. Furthermore, only about 20% of drivers within the rideshare industry are female, resulting in a predominantly white-male rideshare workforce. The need for a rideshare application that can provide the user with the comfort and security they desire is necessary to prevent the industry from dying.

02

Comfort and Security

Driversity considers the user's comfort by giving them options for their drivers.

Driversity is designed to make the user feel safe and secure while embarking on their trip. By specifying the characteristics of the requested driver set by the user's preferences, Driversity filters matches that make the user feel as comfortable as possible. Qualities such as a driver's gender, ethnicity, and religion are taken into account to filter out any drivers that might end up resulting in an unenjoyable and uncomfortable experience for the user.

03

Airport Reliability

Driversity makes air travel easier to manage

One of the features that Driversity offers is the ability to make scheduled rides based off the user's flights. The user won't have to worry about rushing the morning of their flight because Driversity will already have scheduled a driver to come to the user's location and drop them off at the correct destination, allowing for a relaxing and leisurely morning drive to the airport.

04

Research shows...

Passengers feel safer riding with people of the same gender.

Results from our research shows that passengers using a rideshare service are more comfortable with riding with a driver of the same gender. Some users also have different preferences for their drivers depending on the time of day.
